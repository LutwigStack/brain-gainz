# 03 Task And Criteria Emphasis

## Status

`planned`

## Goal

Make the actual learning task visually stronger than verification metadata.

## Scope

- highlight `Что сделать`
- highlight `Что считается зачтено`
- make checklist items feel like criteria, not form rows
- keep required/optional states clear
- keep progress and XP result feedback secondary until result exists

## Done When

- the task prompt is the first meaningful content in the lesson
- criteria are easy to scan
- the main action follows naturally after criteria
